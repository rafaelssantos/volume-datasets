Description: CT scan of the terracotta Stanford Bunny. The greyscale units are Hounsfield units, denoting electron-density of the subject. The data is raw 512x512 slices, unsigned, 12 bit data stored as 16bit (2-byte) pixels. Provided by Terry Yoo, High Performance Computing and Communications, National Library of Medicine, USA.
